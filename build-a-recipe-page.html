Masala Veg Maggi

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making Maggi! This quick and tasty dish is perfect for when you're hungry and need something delicious in just a few minutes. Follow this easy recipe to make classic, comforting Maggi that’s full of flavor and super satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles (with tastemaker)
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 tsp oil or butter (optional)
  • Veggies (optional):
  • 2 tbsp chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped capsicum
  • A few green peas
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • Spices (for extra flavor):
  • ¼ tsp red chili powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ¼ tsp garam masala or Maggi masala-e-magic (optional)
  • Salt to taste (only if needed)
  • Extras (optional):
  • Grated cheese
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • A squeeze of lemon
  • A dash of chaat masala

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tsp oil or butter in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté for a minute until slightly golden.
  3. Add tomatoes, capsicum, peas, and green chili (if using). Cook for 2–3 minutes until the veggies soften.
  4. Pour in 1½ cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  5. Add the Maggi noodle cake and the tastemaker spice mix.
  6. Optional: Add extra spices like red chili powder, turmeric, or garam masala for more flavor.
  7. Cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the noodles are soft and the water is mostly absorbed.
  8. Turn off the heat. Optional: Add grated cheese or a squeeze of lemon.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves or a pinch of chaat masala, if desired.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Masala Maggi!
